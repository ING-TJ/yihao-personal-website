# Translation Review Required

English and Simplified Chinese are the content bases. Arabic, French, Russian, Spanish, Japanese and Korean are complete editorial first drafts and require native-speaker review before formal public launch.

## Review every language

- Long research descriptions and research questions
- Academic status: PhD Candidate / doctoral researcher
- Formal school, university and company names
- Award names, competition names and award levels
- Environmental engineering and water-safety terminology
- “Under Review” versus “In Preparation” publication status
- “晶水默助” and ING product naming
- Tone: rigorous, understated and non-promotional

## Arabic

- Full RTL flow, logical punctuation and mixed Latin/Arabic sequences
- Numerals and years inside award names
- Transliteration of Yihao Bian, Tongji, GIST and Jingshuiji
- Button order and directional language

## French, Russian and Spanish

- Official institutional names rather than literal translations
- Gender, number and academic-title conventions
- Competition and scholarship terminology

## Japanese and Korean

- Official forms of Chinese names and institutions
- Appropriate spacing between Latin abbreviations and local scripts
- Natural academic register rather than direct Chinese word order

Record reviewer name, review date and approved terminology before clearing this document for launch.
